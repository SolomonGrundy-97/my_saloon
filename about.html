Complete

Customer Satisfaction


From the moment they opened the doors to the first SOLOBARBS in 1997 Solomon focused everything he did on the one goal. It meant more than expertly cutting, styling, and colouring hair. He needed to make sure clients were comfortable, got the latest precision cuts and styles, and learned how to care for their hair.

Today, 25 years later, Solomon's legacy carries on in every family owned SOLOBARBS salon. And for every customer we serve. It's a formula that ensures you leave after each visit feeling satisfied with the services and products you - and feeling more Beautiful than ever. That's really what we'er all about.
